How it works

  • The uploaded image or drawing is converted to grayscale and resized to 28×28 pixels.
  • Pixel values are normalized to [0, 1] and, if needed, inverted to match MNIST’s white-on-black style.
  • A CNN trained on the MNIST dataset outputs probabilities for digits 0–9.
  • The digit with the highest probability is shown as the prediction, and the circular gauge shows its confidence.
